FAQs

How does this work?

Home automation is a network of hardware, communication, and electronic interfaces that work to integrate everyday devices with one another via the Internet. Each device has sensors and is connected through WiFi, so you can manage them from your smartphone or tablet whether you're at home, or miles away.

What are the advantages of Home Automation?

Smart homes are very efficient in energy savings.It allows flexible user preferences. With automation, a user can transform almost every aspect of their lifestyle. There are various smart automation options for security like HD CCTV cameras, anti-theft systems and biometric locks, among others.

What are the elements of home automation?

What are the elements of home automation? At the most basic level, home automation systems are made up of three elements: a smart hub, a smartphone application, and a smart device.

What is the concept of home automation system?

“Home automation” refers to the automatic and electronic control of household features, activity, and appliances. In simple terms, it means you can easily control the utilities and features of your home via the Internet to make life more convenient and secure, and even spend less on household bills.

What's the history of home automation?

Home automation began to increase in popularity in the late 1990s and early 2000s as internet technology developed fast and smart homes suddenly became a more affordable option. Domestic technology or 'domotics' was a highly discussed topic as domestic appliances were being combined with computers.
